Welcome to The Allen Group

Who are we?

We are an independent group of corporations, aimed at reducing the world's dependence on fossil fuels. Over the years, We have slowly been converting various Mega companies to use less mining intensive materials in their production. An example of one of our successes is the Apple Company. In 2023, We assisted them by brainstorming new ideas for production materials, Like their all- new Apple watch Series-9®, which features a refined design and a 100% recycled aluminum frame. The use of recycled materials enabled them to acheive their first 100% carbon-neutral device, ahead of their total carbon-neutral goal by 2030.

What Do We Do?

The Allen Group is an energy corporation that is committed to reducing the impact of the manufacturing process on the environment. The company offers a variety of services to help businesses improve their energy efficiency and reduce their carbon footprint. One of the Allen Group's most popular services is its energy audit program.
During an energy audit, a team of experts will assess a business's energy usage and identify areas where savings can be made. The Allen Group can also help businesses implement energy-saving measures, such as installing new lighting or insulation. In addition to its energy audit program, the Allen Group also offers a variety of other services to help businesses reduce their environmental impact. These services include: Renewable energy consulting: The Allen Group can help businesses develop and implement plans to use renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power. Carbon offset programs: The Allen Group can help businesses offset their carbon emissions by investing in projects that reduce greenhouse gases. Green building consulting: The Allen Group can help businesses design and build green buildings that are more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly.
The Allen Group is committed to helping businesses reduce their environmental impact. The company's services are helping businesses save money and protect the planet. Here are some of the Allen Group's recent successes: Helped a manufacturing plant reduce its energy consumption by 20%. Advised a company on how to offset its carbon emissions by 100%. Designed a green building that is expected to save its owner $1 million in energy costs over its lifetime. The Allen Group is a leader in the field of sustainable manufacturing. The company's services are helping businesses reduce their environmental impact and save money.
